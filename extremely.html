EXTREMELY ONLINE 2021

OVERVIEW
"extremely online" is an online showcase, a collaborative project from Curating the Digital studio, RMIT's Digital Media program, that connect people together through digital artwork in the middle of the pandemic to celebrate and share each others' creativity.
OBJECTIVES
  • Holding page with countdown feature to promote the event.
  • Website as the main infrastructure to showcase the selected works for the online exhibition.
  • Clean and organised interface with sections for different types of design work, as well as the thought for accessibility.
PROCESS
For this collaborative project, I worked as a part of the web design/development team as an front-end developer. Our team's responsibility was to design the UX/UI and develop the holding page to promote the online event, as well as the website, one of the most important for of the event, to feature every work that all the teams of the project worked on, as well as display every selected artwork for the exhibition.
