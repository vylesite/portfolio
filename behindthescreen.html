BEHIND THE SCREEN

OVERVIEW
"Behind The Screen" is a informative website which provides people with information about cyberbullying, its dangerous effects, data, and some help information to raise awareness and bring people the knowledge to deal with cyberbullying themselves or help others. Visiting the project, viewers will be taken through different parts to see different types of information provided. The name "Behind The Screen" of the projects alludes to a digital crime that can happen behind the screen of technological devices, cyberbullying. The messages and the goal of the webpage were supported by the colorful but cruel visual element, the storytelling, hyperlinks, etc.
Cyberbullying is bullying with the use of digital technologies. It is repeated behavior that is done on purpose to anger, threat, shame who targeted. The danger of cyberbullying is getting bigger than ever before because of the growth of information technology and the prevalence of social media. Like face-to-face bullying, cyberbullying can hurt mentally, physically, and emotionally and left permanent psychological scars on the victims. Therefore, this is the wicked problem that "Behind The Screen" is targeted.
OBJECTIVES
  • Promote awareness of an issue and facilitate user/audience engagement with it
  • Open up people’s feelings and attitudes towards a topic via appeal to their human emotion.
  • Provide facts and figures to tell a compelling story and engage with the audience intellectually as well as an emotionally.
Motion graphics as demo video for Behind The Screen's user interface.
RESULT
The title “behind the screen” appears with glitching effect, which gives not only the feeling of cyberspace but also reminds people of negative things about technology, including cyberbullying. In the middle of the screen is a hand raising asking for help to get away from the danger of technology for users to click on to explore further.
The chat box takes audiences through the story of a character, a victim of cyberbullying, for them to understand and empathise.
The illustrations include the combination of technological devices with weapons and things to indicate death, which alludes to the danger of technology, with cyberbullying being a part of it, for example, the knife coming out of the computer, a hand asking for help from the smartphone, being depressed and suiciding because of social media, and the computer mouse connects with a rope also indicate suicide.
The blue background gets lighter on scrolling down to show the aim also the hope with making the problem of only bullying get better.
